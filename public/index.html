📖 SPIFF Rules & Guide
The Objective
Maximize your bank balance by the end of Q2 FY27. Your bank starts at $100,000. Revenue from closed won deals (multiplied by your token weight) is added to your bank. Deals that close lost subtract their raw value. The SE with the highest bank at quarter end wins.
Tokens
  • Each SE starts with 10 tokens numbered 1–10.
  • Token number = multiplier. Token 10 = 10x, Token 9 = 9x, down to Token 1 = 1x.
  • Assign each token to one opportunity. Only one token per opportunity.
  • When a deal closes won, your bank earns: deal value × token multiplier.
  • When a deal closes lost, your bank loses the raw deal value (no multiplier).
  • Any open deal at quarter end is automatically marked closed lost.
  • Tokens assigned to closed won or closed lost deals are burned — they cannot be reassigned or sold.
Reassignment Windows
  • You may reassign a token to a different opportunity twice per quarter.
  • Reassignments cost $100,000 each and are only allowed during two windows:
  • Window 1: June 1–5 (start of Month 2)
  • Window 2: July 1–5 (start of Month 3)
  • Outside these windows, token assignments are locked.
  • Burned tokens (on closed deals) cannot be reassigned even during a window.
Token Market
  • SEs can negotiate token trades with each other at any agreed price.
  • Contact the Admin to execute a trade — you agree on a price, the admin logs it.
  • The buyer's bank is charged the agreed price; the seller's bank is credited.
  • Banks cannot go negative. If you can't afford it, the trade is blocked.
  • Burned tokens cannot be sold.
  • All trades are logged and visible to everyone.
Bank Balance
  • Starts at $100,000.
  • Increases with: closed won game revenue, token sale proceeds.
  • Decreases with: closed lost raw value, reassignment fees, token purchases.
  • Bank can never go below $0.
  • Final ranking is determined by bank balance at quarter end.
End of Quarter
When the admin closes the quarter, all open opportunities with tokens assigned are automatically moved to Closed Lost, and their deal values are deducted from the respective SE's bank. Plan accordingly — don't leave risky opps open!
